Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

NeoGreen Energy (Pvt.) Ltd. ("NeoGreen", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, services, or our team.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Energy usage data and property information for system sizing
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely via third-party gateways)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Communication records (emails, chat transcripts, service requests)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide solar installation, maintenance, and support services
  • To generate customized proposals and solar performance reports
  • To process payments and manage warranties
  • To send updates about products, promotions, and regulatory changes
  • To improve our website and service quality

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information with authorized service partners (e.g., installation contractors, ABCD for net metering) strictly to fulfill your service agreement. All partners are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and restricted access controls. However, no digital transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your account credentials.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@neogreen.pk or call +92-21-111-NEO-GRN.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about solar energy and NeoGreen's services.

Solar system costs vary by size and type. A typical 5kW residential system costs between PKR 700,000–900,000 including installation, net metering, and 1-year AMC. Our solar calculator can give you a personalized estimate based on your consumption.
Net metering allows your solar system to feed excess electricity back to the grid. Your electricity meter runs forward when you consume grid power and backward when your solar system generates surplus. DISCO credits your account for the exported units, reducing your monthly bill significantly.
A standard residential rooftop installation takes 1–3 days. Commercial projects may take 1–2 weeks. Net metering approval from DISCO typically takes an additional 4–8 weeks, during which your system operates off-grid or as a backup system.
Solar panels carry a 25-year linear performance warranty and 10-year product warranty. Inverters come with a 5–10 year manufacturer warranty. Lithium-ion batteries have a 10-year or 6,000-cycle warranty. NeoGreen also provides a 1-year comprehensive AMC on all installations.
Yes! With a hybrid inverter and battery storage system, your solar setup can provide uninterrupted power during load shedding. The batteries charge from solar during the day and power your home or office when the grid goes down.
System sizing depends on your average monthly electricity consumption (units/kWh), available roof space, and budget. Use our free Solar Calculator or speak with our energy consultants for a detailed site assessment and customized proposal.
Yes, NeoGreen partners with leading banks to offer easy installment plans (0% markup on select schemes). We also assist with government solar loans and Green Finance initiatives. Contact our team to explore financing options suited to your budget.
Solar panels require minimal maintenance — mainly periodic cleaning to remove dust. We recommend professional maintenance every 6 months. Our annual maintenance contracts (AMC) include panel cleaning, system health checks, inverter inspection, and remote monitoring support.
